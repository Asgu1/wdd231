Individual Project Site Plan

Prepared by: Aarón Gutiérrez

Section 1

Site Name

Auto Care Workshop

Reason: The name Auto Care Workshop is direct, professional, and immediately communicates what the business offers — automotive care and repair services. It is easy to remember, search-friendly, and conveys a sense of hands-on, reliable service that customers expect from a local mechanic shop.

Optional domain: autocareworkshop.com

Section 2

Site Purpose

Auto Care Workshop is a local auto repair shop website designed to showcase the business's services, build customer trust, and make it easy for clients to get in touch. The site includes three pages: a home page with a welcome section, featured services, and business hours; a services page listing available repairs — such as oil changes, brake inspection, engine diagnostics, and tire rotation — with descriptions and pricing; and a contact page with an appointment scheduling form, the shop's location, phone number, and social media links. Service data is loaded dynamically from a local JSON file to generate cards for each repair offering.

Section 3

Scenarios

These questions represent typical visitors to the site and guide the site's content decisions:

"What types of car repairs does this shop offer, and how much do they typically cost?"
"How can I schedule an appointment for an oil change or brake inspection?"
"Where is the shop located and what are the business hours?"

Section 4

Color Scheme

The palette reflects the professional and reliable feel of an automotive workshop — deep navy conveys trust, amber adds energy and warmth, and the neutral background keeps the focus on content.

Deep Navy #1B3A5C Header, nav, section headings, buttons
Warm Amber #C17F3E Accents, links, badges, highlights, CTA borders
Warm Cream #F7F4EF Page background, card backgrounds, subtle fills
Near Black #2D2D2D Body text, paragraphs, footer text

Section 5

Typography

Two typefaces are selected — one serif for character and one sans-serif for readability.

Font 1 — Playfair Display (Google Fonts)

Auto Care Workshop

Used for: Page title (h1), section headings (h2), hero text, site logo. Provides editorial, refined character.

Font 2 — DM Sans (Google Fonts)

A lightweight, modern sans-serif ideal for body copy and navigation. Highly legible at all sizes and optimized for screen reading.

Used for: Body text, navigation links, form labels, captions, metadata. Clean and accessible at all weights.
